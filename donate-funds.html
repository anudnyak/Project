How To Donate Funds

Canada Helps

It takes less than 5 minutes. We particularly appreciate monthly donations because they make it easier for us to budget for wildlife needs over longer periods. We can also take charge card contributions over the phone at (778) 428-1990 or you can send or drop off a cheque if that is more convenient.

Donate Now

E-Transfer

If e-transferring is more convenient, please send to info@marswildliferescue.com Please include your full name and address if a tax receipt is required and indicate that the e-transfer is for donation. Thank you!

Gift-In-Kind

One method of gifting that we could not do without is the gift-in-kind. Building materials, labour, food for birds and animals, hospital supplies, veterinary expenses, crafting, caregiving, and office administration are just some of the many areas where the gift of giving need not be cash. Please contact us if you have special ways you’d like to give.

Refundables

Refundable bottles and cans may be donated to MARS accounts at:

  • Courtenay Return-It Depot at 493 Puntledge Rd. (#135)
  • Island Return-It 1580-F Willow Street, Campbell River

We have an EXPRESS ACCOUNT! (# 778-428-2000)

OR bring your refundables directly to the green collection shed across from the Visitor Centre at 1331 Williams Beach Rd. No recycling, please!

Donate a car

Donate a Car Canada accepts vehicle donations for MARS Wildlife Rescue Centre. Free towing is provided in most areas across Canada, or you can drop off your vehicle to maximize your donation.

Adopt an Ambassador Bird

Our non-releasable bird ambassadors represent not only their species and our wildlife rescue, but all wildlife at risk. To support the work we do, we welcome you to symbolically adopt one of these beautful birds for one month, for $100.

What to do: Look at all the ambassadors on this page. Choose the bird you’d like to adopt, then click the ADOPT button to get to the Canada Helps page. Once there,

  • select donation amount ($100)
  • under FUND, select choice 3
  • under Write a private message to us, record the bird’s name, and the recipient’s name and address.
  • The remaining information on the page will allow you to pay and receive your tax receipt.
