Donate Goods

Donations can be dropped off at the MARS Wildlife Hospital at 1331 Williams Beach Road, Merville, or mailed to P.O. Box 415, Merville, B.C., V0R 2M0

Current Needs

We are in particular need of

  • Leafy greens
  • Vegetables: things like yams/sweet potatoes, broccoli, cauliflower, peppers, Brussel sprouts (no regular potatoes)
  • Cuttle bone (these are used by squirrels and big beaked birds like Evening Grosbeaks)

We also need

  • Unscented bleach
  • Dish soap
  • Puppy milk replacer: Esbilac
  • Kitten milk replacer: KMR

Everyday Needs

The small things really help, and we are always on the lookout for bits and pieces, odds and ends, such as:

  • Linens
    • Washcloths
    • Towels
    • Fitted sheets
    • Flat sheets
    • Pillow cases
    • Comforters
    • Hospital pads
    • Baby bird nests
    • Small dog/cat beds
    • Fleece / warmie squares
    • Carpet squares
  • Toilet paper
  • Facial tissues/Kleenex
  • White paper towel
  • Large zip-loc bags
  • Garbage bags (small kitchen bags and large black ones)
  • Nitrile or latex exam gloves (size medium)
  • Human vitamins or supplements (Vitamin B1, Vitamin D, Vitamin A, Vitamin E capsules, Cod Liver Oil with Vitamin A & D, calcium, etc)
  • Pet medications (Metacam, Clindamycin/Antirobe, Clavaseptin, Clavamox, Baytril, etc.)
  • Large bird and critter cages (not cracked, broken, or rusted)
  • Toddler/pet safe toys
  • Stuffed animals (without pellets or beans)
  • Any chewable/edible toys for small mammals and birds (available at Featheredaddictions.com)
